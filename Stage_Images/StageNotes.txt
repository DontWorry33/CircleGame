The Box Formulae
	The box contains three different charges, each in a different box. The objective is to get the Baker to hit any of those charges and become ‘charged’. At which point, you have two* tries to make it to box 7. If you fail, the charge is lost.

	• Total Charge starts at 3. Increments by 1 for every switch hit.
	• Inert Charge == current switch hit. 1, 2 or 3.
	
	• Charge Placement Formula: [(Total•1)-Inert]%9, [(Total•2)-Inert]%9, [(Total•3)-Inert]%9
	• Baker Placement Formula: [Current Position + Inert]%9
